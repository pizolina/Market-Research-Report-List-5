<p><p>Biometric service market can be categorized into two types based on the number of biometric modalities used for identity verification - Unimodal mode and Multimodal mode. Unimodal mode refers to using only one biometric modality, such as fingerprint or facial recognition, for authentication purposes. On the other hand, Multimodal mode involves using multiple biometric modalities, like fingerprint and iris scan, for a more reliable and secure identification process. Both modes cater to different needs and requirements of organizations depending on the level of security needed for their operations.</p></p>
