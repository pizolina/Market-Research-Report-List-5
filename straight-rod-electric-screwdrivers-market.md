<p><p>Straight rod electric screwdrivers are divided into two types: L Type and T Type market. They are commonly used in situations where precision and control are important. On the other hand, T Type screwdrivers have a handle that forms a T shape with the tool, allowing for easier access to tight spaces and corners. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
