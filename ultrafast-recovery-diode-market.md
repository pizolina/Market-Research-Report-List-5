<p><p>Ultrafast recovery diodes can be classified into two types: single tube and double tube. In single tube diodes, the entire diode is contained within a single tube package, providing compactness and easy integration into circuits. On the other hand, double tube diodes feature two separate tubes connected within a single package, offering improved electrical performance and reliability. Both types cater to different market needs, with single tube diodes being favored for compact applications and double tube diodes for enhanced performance requirements.</p></p>
