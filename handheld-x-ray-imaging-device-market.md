<p><p>The Handheld X Ray Imaging Device market is primarily divided into two types - Direct Radiography (DR) and Computed Radiography (CR). DR systems use digital detectors to capture X-ray images in real-time, offering high image quality and faster image processing. CR systems, on the other hand, use imaging plates to capture X-ray images which are then processed digitally. While both types offer different technological approaches, they both aim to provide efficient and accurate diagnostic imaging solutions.</p></p>
