<p><p>Automated Vacuum Waste Collection Systems can be categorised into two types: overground and underground. Overground systems are installed above the ground level and typically use a network of underground pipes to transport waste to a central collection point. Underground systems, on the other hand, are installed beneath the ground level and use vacuum technology to transport waste through underground pipes. Both types offer efficient and convenient waste collection solutions for urban environments, with underground systems often preferred for their space-saving and aesthetic benefits.</p></p>
