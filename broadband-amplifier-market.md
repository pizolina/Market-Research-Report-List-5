<p><p>Broadband amplifiers are divided into various market types based on their specific applications. Drive amplifiers are designed to provide input signal amplification and drive power to other components in a system. Power amplifiers are used to increase the amplitude of the signal to drive higher power outputs. Other types include distribution amplifiers, which are used to split and amplify signals to multiple outputs, and low noise amplifiers, which are designed to amplify weak signals while minimizing added noise. Each type serves a specific purpose in amplifying signals across a broad frequency range.</p></p>
