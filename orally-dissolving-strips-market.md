<p><p>Orally dissolving strips market types include Orodispersible Films and Oromucosal Films. Orodispersible Films are designed to dissolve quickly in the mouth, providing a convenient and discreet way to deliver medications. They are commonly used for pain relief, allergy treatment, and vitamin supplements. Oromucosal Films are similar, but are specifically formulated to adhere to the mucous membranes in the mouth, allowing for controlled release of medication. Both types of films offer a convenient alternative to traditional oral medications and are becoming increasingly popular in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
