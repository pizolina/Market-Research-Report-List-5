Fluorosilicone Elastomers offer improved flexibility and low-temperature performance, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Perfluorocarbon Elastomers provide exceptional resistance to extreme temperatures, chemicals, and fuels, making them ideal for use in harsh industrial environments.
