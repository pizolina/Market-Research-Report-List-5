<p><ul><li>Vertical Jet Pump</li><li>Horizontal Jet Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet pumps are commonly used in both residential and industrial applications. Vertical jet pumps are designed for wells with low water levels, where the jet assembly is submerged deep in the well. Horizontal jet pumps are typically used in situations where the water source is shallow, such as a lake or stream. Both types of jet pumps work by using a combination of centrifugal force and suction to draw water into the pump and then deliver it to the desired location.</p></p>
