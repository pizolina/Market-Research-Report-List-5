<p><p>On-Board Magnetic Sensor Market types include Hall Effect Sensors, Magnetoresistive Sensors, SQUID Sensors, and others. Hall Effect Sensors utilize the Hall Effect to measure magnetic fields. Magnetoresistive Sensors detect changes in resistance when exposed to magnetic fields. SQUID Sensors are highly sensitive to magnetic fields and are commonly used in scientific research. Other types of on-board magnetic sensors may include fluxgate sensors, magneto-impedance sensors, and giant magnetoresistive sensors, each with unique operating principles and applications.</p></p>
