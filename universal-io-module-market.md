<p><p>Universal IO Modules are versatile components used in various industries for processing input and output signals. Digital IO Modules are designed to handle binary data signals, while Analog IO Modules are capable of processing continuous variable signals. Digital IO Modules are commonly used for tasks such as controlling relays or reading switch positions, while Analog IO Modules are used for tasks that require precise measurement and control of variables such as temperature, pressure, or voltage. Both types of modules play a crucial role in automation and control systems.</p></p>
