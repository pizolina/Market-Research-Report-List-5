<p><p>Carbon Fibre Tape Market Types include Prepreg Tape and Dry Tape. Prepreg Tape is a type of carbon fibre tape that comes pre-impregnated with resin, making it easier to handle and allowing for better control over the curing process. Dry Tape, on the other hand, is a form of carbon fibre tape that does not contain any resin, requiring the resin to be applied separately during the manufacturing process. Both types of carbon fibre tape offer unique advantages and are commonly used in various industries for their lightweight and high strength properties.</p></p>
