<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Liquid Oxygen Pump</li><li>Reciprocating Liquid Oxygen Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid oxygen pump market consists of two main types: centrifugal liquid oxygen pumps and reciprocating liquid oxygen pumps. Centrifugal pumps use a rotating impeller to create a centrifugal force that moves the oxygen through the system. Reciprocating pumps, on the other hand, use a piston to move the liquid oxygen in a back and forth motion. Both types of pumps are used in various industries where liquid oxygen is required, such as medical applications, aerospace, and industrial processes.</p></p>
