<p><p>B-type Ultrasound Diagnostic System market types refer to different scanning methods used in ultrasound imaging. Fanshaped scanning involves using a transducer to emit ultrasound waves in a fan-shaped pattern, allowing for a broader view of the internal organs. Linear scanning utilizes a probe that moves in a straight line to capture detailed images of specific areas. Mixed type market combines these two scanning methods to provide a comprehensive and detailed analysis of the patient's condition, offering a versatile approach to ultrasound diagnostics.</p></p>
