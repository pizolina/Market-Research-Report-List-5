<p><p>Ship loaders are equipment used to load bulk materials such as grain, coal, and ore onto ships for transportation. There are two main types of ship loaders: mobile ship loaders and stationary ship loaders. </p><p>Mobile ship loaders are movable and can be easily transported to different locations for loading operations. They offer flexibility and efficiency in loading various types of cargo. </p><p>Stationary ship loaders, on the other hand, are fixed in one place and are generally used for continuous loading operations at ports or terminals. They are typically larger in size and have higher loading capacities compared to mobile ship loaders.</p></p>
