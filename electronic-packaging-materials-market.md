<p><p>Electronic packaging materials are categorized into three main types: Metal Packages, Plastic Packages, and Ceramic Packages. Metal packages are commonly used due to their high thermal conductivity and good electrical properties. Plastic packages are cost-effective and lightweight, making them suitable for various applications. Ceramic packages provide excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-power electronic devices. Each type of packaging material offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the electronic component being packaged.</p></p>
