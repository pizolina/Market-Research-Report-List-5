<p><ul><li>Contact Equipment</li><li>Contactless Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical metrology equipment includes two main types: Contact Equipment and Contactless Equipment. Contact Equipment involves physically touching the object being measured, typically using probes or sensors. This type is suitable for measuring hard surfaces or objects with complex geometries. Contactless Equipment, on the other hand, employs methods such as laser scanning or X-ray imaging to measure objects without making physical contact. This type is ideal for delicate or sensitive surfaces where physical contact may cause damage. Both types play a crucial role in various industries for precise and accurate measurements.</p></p>
