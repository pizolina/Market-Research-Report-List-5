<p><p>Fiber Laser Chips are essential components in fiber optics communication systems. The three main types are DFB (Distributed Feedback) Laser Chips, VCSEL (Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser) Chips, and EML (Electro-absorption Modulated Laser) Chips. DFB chips are known for their single-frequency output and high stability. VCSEL chips offer high power efficiency and are commonly used in short-distance applications. EML chips combine laser and modulator functions for high-speed data transmission. Each type of fiber laser chip has unique advantages and is used in different applications based on their characteristics.</p></p>
