<p><p>Thermoformers are machines used for shaping plastic materials into desired forms. The market for thermoformers can be classified into three types based on operation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual thermoformers require human intervention for every step of the process. Semi-automatic thermoformers involve some level of automation, but still require operator control. Fully automatic thermoformers have automated systems for heating, forming, and cutting processes, minimizing the need for human intervention. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency and production capacity to meet different industry needs.</p></p>
