<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Oil-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet inks are classified into three main types based on their composition: water-based, oil-based, and solvent-based. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and suitable for printing on various paper types. Oil-based inks are known for their durability and are commonly used for printing on non-porous materials like plastic and metal. Solvent-based inks are ideal for outdoor applications as they are resistant to fading and water. Each type has its own specific uses and benefits in the inkjet inks market.</p></p>
