<p><p>Reactive silencers are used in various industries to reduce noise levels generated by equipment or machinery. Two common types of reactive silencers are resonant silencers and expansion silencers. Resonant silencers utilize tuned chambers to reduce specific frequencies of noise, while expansion silencers use multiple expansion chambers to reflect and absorb noise energy. Both types of silencers are designed to effectively mitigate noise pollution and create a quieter environment for workers and surrounding areas.</p></p>
