<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing Printers are advanced machines that use metal powders to create three-dimensional objects layer by layer. The market for these printers can be classified into different types based on the technology used. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) printers use a high-energy laser to melt and fuse metal powders together. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) printers use an electron beam to melt and solidify metal powders. Other types of metal powder additive manufacturing printers may utilize different techniques for melting and fusing metal powders.</p></p>
