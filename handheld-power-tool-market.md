<p><p>Handheld power tools are categorized into three main types based on their power source – electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Electric power tools are powered by electricity and are commonly used for household and industrial applications. Pneumatic power tools are powered by compressed air and are widely used in construction and automotive industries. Hydraulic power tools are powered by hydraulic systems and are known for their high force output, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks in industries such as mining and construction.</p></p>
