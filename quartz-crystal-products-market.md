<p><p>Quartz crystal products are commonly available in two main market types: DIP type and SMD type. DIP type products are designed with dual in-line package technology, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on PCBs. On the other hand, SMD type products feature surface mount device technology, allowing for easy and compact mounting on circuit boards. Both types offer high accuracy and stability in frequency control applications, catering to various industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
