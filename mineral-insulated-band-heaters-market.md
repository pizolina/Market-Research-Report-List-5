High Thermally Conductive band heaters are designed to quickly and efficiently transfer heat to the target surface, making them ideal for applications requiring rapid heating or high temperatures. On the other hand, Low Thermally Conductive band heaters are suitable for applications where a slower, more gradual heating process is needed, or when insulation is important to prevent heat loss.</p></p>
