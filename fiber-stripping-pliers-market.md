<p><p>Fiber stripping pliers come in two main types: single hole and three holes. Single hole pliers are designed for stripping the outer jacket of fiber optic cables, while three-hole pliers are used for stripping the outer jacket, buffer, and coating of the cable. Single hole pliers are simpler in design and more suitable for basic stripping tasks, while three-hole pliers offer more versatility and precision for intricate fiber optic cable stripping processes. Both types cater to different needs in the fiber optic industry.</p></p>
