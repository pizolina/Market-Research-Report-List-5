<p><strong>The Radiant Electric Floor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant Electric Floor Heating systems utilize electric cables to generate heat directly beneath the floor surface, providing efficient and uniform heat distribution. This technology is popular in residential and commercial settings due to its ease of installation and low maintenance requirements. On the other hand, Hydronic Radiant Floor Heating systems use hot water circulated through tubes beneath the floor to heat the space. This method is known for its energy efficiency and ability to maintain consistent warmth throughout the room. Both electric and hydronic systems offer customizable heating solutions for various applications.</p></p>
