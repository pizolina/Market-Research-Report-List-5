<p><p>Electric Vehicles Wireless Charging market includes two types of technologies: Electromagnetic Induction and Magnetic Resonance. Electromagnetic Induction technology uses a coil in the ground to create a magnetic field that transfers energy to a receiver coil on the vehicle. Magnetic Resonance technology, on the other hand, uses resonant coils to transfer energy wirelessly over longer distances, allowing for more flexibility in parking and alignment. Both technologies are aimed at providing convenient and efficient charging solutions for electric vehicles.</p></p>
