<p><p>The Antilock Brake System (ABS) market is segmented into four types based on the number of channels: Four Channel, Three Channel, Two Channel, and One Channel. Four Channel ABS involves a sensor on each wheel to independently control braking pressure. Three Channel ABS has three sensors that control pressure for three wheels, while Two Channel ABS has two sensors that control opposite wheels. One Channel ABS uses a single sensor to control braking pressure for all wheels simultaneously. Each type offers varying levels of control and safety in different driving conditions.</p></p>
