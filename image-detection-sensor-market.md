<p><p>Image detection sensors are used in various industries for detecting and capturing images. The three main types of image detection sensors are CCD (charge-coupled device), CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor), and thermal sensors. CCD sensors are known for their high-quality images and low light sensitivity. CMOS sensors are more commonly used in consumer electronics due to their low power consumption. Thermal sensors detect heat signatures and are commonly used in night vision and security applications. Each type of sensor has its own unique advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
