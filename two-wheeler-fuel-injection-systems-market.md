<p><p>Two-wheeler fuel injection systems are used in motorcycles and scooters to deliver fuel efficiently to the engine. There are two main types of fuel injection systems - direct fuel injection system and port fuel injection system. </p><p>Direct fuel injection system delivers fuel directly into the combustion chamber, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and performance. </p><p>Port fuel injection system delivers fuel to the intake port of the cylinder, providing a more uniform air-fuel mixture. Both systems aim to improve the overall efficiency and performance of two-wheeler engines.</p></p>
