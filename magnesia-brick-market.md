<p><p>Magnesia bricks are commonly classified into four types: MZ-82, MZ-87, MZ-89, and MZ-91, based on their magnesia content and thermal properties. MZ-82 bricks have a lower magnesia content and are suitable for lower temperature applications. MZ-87 bricks have a higher magnesia content and are ideal for medium temperature environments. MZ-89 bricks are designed for high temperature operations, while MZ-91 bricks are the most high-grade option with superior resistance to thermal shock. Each type of magnesia brick caters to specific temperature requirements in various industrial settings.</p></p>
