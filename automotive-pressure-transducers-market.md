<p><strong>The Automotive Pressure Transducers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gauge Pressure</li><li>Absolute Pressure</li><li>Sealed Pressure</li><li>Vacuum Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Pressure Transducers are used to measure different types of pressure in vehicles. Gauge Pressure transducers measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Absolute Pressure transducers measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum. Sealed Pressure transducers are sealed and measure pressure within a specific system. Vacuum Pressure transducers measure pressure below atmospheric pressure. Each type of transducer is designed for specific applications in the automotive industry, providing accurate pressure measurements for various systems such as engines, brakes, and fuel systems.</p></p>
