<p><p>LDS (Laser Direct Structuring) and LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) are two common types of plastic antenna elements used in the market. LDS antennas are created by using a laser to directly structure the plastic material, allowing for complex antenna designs. LCP antennas are made from a type of plastic that is highly temperature and moisture resistant, making them ideal for harsh environments. The Modified PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) market segment caters to antennas made from a modified form of PPS plastic, offering a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
