<p><strong>The Surge Protection Diode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ESD (Electro Static Discharge)</li><li>TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surge Protection Diode Market includes two main types: ESD (Electro Static Discharge) and TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) diodes. ESD diodes protect electronic components from damage caused by static electricity discharges, which can occur during handling or manufacturing processes. TVS diodes protect against transient voltage spikes in power lines or communication networks, preventing damage from lightning strikes, switching transients, or other electrical disturbances. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding sensitive electronics from potentially harmful voltage surges.</p></p>
