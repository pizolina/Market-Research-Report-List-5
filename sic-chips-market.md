<p><p>SiC chips are increasingly being used in various industries due to their superior thermal conductivity and high breakdown voltage. Two main types of SiC chips are SiC Trench Gate Chips and SiC Planar Gate Chips. SiC Trench Gate Chips offer lower on-resistance and reduced switching losses, making them ideal for high-power applications. On the other hand, SiC Planar Gate Chips are known for their simpler structure and ease of manufacturing, making them suitable for lower-power applications. Both types of SiC chips cater to different market needs and are driving the growth of the SiC chips market.</p></p>
