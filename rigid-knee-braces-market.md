<p><p>Rigid knee braces market is segmented into four main types: prophylactic braces, functional braces, rehabilitative braces, and unloader braces. Prophylactic braces are designed to prevent knee injuries during sports activities. Functional braces provide support for those with existing knee injuries. Rehabilitative braces are used post-injury to aid in the recovery process. Unloader braces are designed to relieve pressure on the knee joint for individuals with arthritis or other degenerative conditions. Each type of brace serves a specific purpose in supporting and protecting the knee.</p></p>
