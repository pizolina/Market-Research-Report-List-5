<p><ul><li>Frame Mount Bike Rack</li><li>Fork Mount Bike Rack</li><li>Wheel Mount Bike Rack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roof bike racks are classified into three main types: frame mount, fork mount, and wheel mount. Frame mount racks secure the bike by clamping onto the bike's frame, making them versatile for different bike sizes and styles. Fork mount racks secure the bike by attaching to the front fork, providing a more stable and secure option for higher speed or rough terrain. Wheel mount racks secure the bike by the wheels, offering a convenient and easy-to-use option for quick loading and unloading. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of cyclists.</p></p>
