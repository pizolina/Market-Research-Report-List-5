<p><p>Aircraft variable pitch propellers come in various types to cater to different aircraft and operating needs. Controllable pitch propellers allow pilots to manually adjust the pitch for optimal performance. Constant speed propellers automatically adjust pitch to maintain a consistent RPM. Full feathering propellers can rotate to a flat position to reduce drag in case of engine failure. Other types of variable pitch propellers include hybrid designs and electrically operated models, offering further versatility in aircraft operations.</p></p>
