<p><p>Threaded fasteners are commonly used in aircraft construction and maintenance, with bolts, nuts, and screws being the most common types. They allow for easy disassembly and reassembly of components, making them ideal for applications that require frequent maintenance. Non-threaded fasteners, such as rivets and pins, provide a secure and permanent fastening solution in areas where vibration and stress are a concern. Both types of fasteners play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of aircraft structures.</p></p>
