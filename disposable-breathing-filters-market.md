<p><p>Disposable Breathing Filters market is segmented into two main types: Mechanical Filters and Electrostatic Filters. Mechanical filters function by trapping particles in a physical barrier, while electrostatic filters work by using an electric charge to attract particles. Mechanical filters are commonly used in healthcare settings to prevent the spread of infections, while electrostatic filters are preferred for their ability to remove smaller particles and provide enhanced filtration efficiency. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining clean and safe indoor air quality.</p></p>
