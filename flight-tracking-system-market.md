<p><ul><li>ADS-B</li><li>FANS</li><li>PFTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flight Tracking System market consists of three main types: ADS-B, FANS, and PFTS. ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast) technology relies on aircraft broadcasting their position to ground stations and other aircraft, enabling real-time tracking. FANS (Future Air Navigation System) integrates communication, navigation, and surveillance capabilities for more efficient and secure air traffic management. PFTS (Portable Flight Tracking System) provides portable tracking solutions for smaller aircraft or remote locations, enhancing safety and operational efficiency. These systems play a crucial role in enhancing aviation safety and efficiency.</p></p>
