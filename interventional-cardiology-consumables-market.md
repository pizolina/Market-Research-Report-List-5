<p><p>Interventional cardiology consumables market includes products such as coronary stents, PTCA balloon catheters, guide wires, and other devices used in minimally invasive cardiac procedures. Coronary stents are small mesh tubes used to treat blockages in the arteries, while PTCA balloon catheters are used to open narrowed vessels. Guide wires help navigate the catheter through the blood vessels. Other consumables may include embolic protection devices, atherectomy devices, and vascular closure devices. These products are essential for performing various interventional cardiology procedures to treat heart disease.</p></p>
