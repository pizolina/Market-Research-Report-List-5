<p><p>Self-adhesive materials are broadly classified into two main types: Rubber Type and Acrylic Type. Rubber Type self-adhesive materials are known for their flexibility, durability, and strong adhesion properties. They are commonly used in applications where a strong bond is required. On the other hand, Acrylic Type self-adhesive materials are recognized for their weather resistance, transparency, and long-term stability. They are often preferred for outdoor applications and where clarity is important. Both types have their unique characteristics that cater to different market needs.</p></p>
