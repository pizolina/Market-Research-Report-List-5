<p><p>Pedestrian pallet trucks are available in two main types: manual and electric. Manual pallet trucks are operated by pushing or pulling a handle to lift and move loads, while electric pallet trucks are powered by a battery-operated motor. Manual pallet trucks are typically more affordable and easier to use for light-duty applications, while electric pallet trucks are ideal for heavier loads and frequent use. Both types offer efficiency and convenience for moving pallets in warehouses, logistics centers, and commercial settings.</p></p>
