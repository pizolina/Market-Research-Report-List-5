<p><ul><li>Incremental Hollow Shaft Encoder</li><li>Absolute Hollow Shaft Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow Shaft Encoders are typically available in two main types, Incremental and Absolute. Incremental Hollow Shaft Encoders produce a series of pulses as the shaft rotates, allowing for position and speed measurement. Absolute Hollow Shaft Encoders provide a unique binary code for each position of the shaft, enabling precise and accurate position tracking. Both types of encoders are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and robotics for applications requiring accurate motion control and position monitoring.</p></p>
