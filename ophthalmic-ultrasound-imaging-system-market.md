<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems are used in eye care to visualize and diagnose various eye conditions. The different types of ultrasound imaging systems include A-Scan, which measures the length of the eye and aids in calculating intraocular lens power, B-Scan, which provides cross-sectional images of the eye's internal structures, Combined Scan which combines A-Scan and B-Scan capabilities, Pachymeter which measures corneal thickness, and Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM) which provides high-resolution images of the anterior segment of the eye for detailed examination.</p></p>
