<p><p>Airless sprayer market is categorized into different types based on their power source. Pneumatic airless sprayers are powered by compressed air, providing high pressure for paint application. Electric airless sprayers are powered by electricity, offering more convenience and ease of use. Thermal airless sprayers use heat to atomize the paint, resulting in a smoother finish. Other types of airless sprayers include hydraulic airless sprayers and gas-powered airless sprayers, each offering unique features and benefits for various applications.</p></p>
