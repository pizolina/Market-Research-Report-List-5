<p><p>Spinal retractors are surgical instruments used during spinal surgeries to hold soft tissues and organs out of the way, providing a clear view and access to the surgical site. Blade retractors have sharp edges that help in cutting through tissues, while tubular retractors have a tubular design allowing for a more minimally invasive approach. Blade retractors are commonly used in open surgeries, while tubular retractors are favored in minimally invasive procedures due to their smaller incisions and reduced tissue trauma.</p></p>
