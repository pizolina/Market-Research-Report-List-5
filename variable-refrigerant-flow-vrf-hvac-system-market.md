<p><p>Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) HVAC System market is segmented into 2 pipe systems and 3 pipe systems. In 2 pipe systems, a single refrigerant pipe is used for both heating and cooling, allowing for more flexibility in building design. In contrast, 3 pipe systems have separate pipes for heating and cooling, providing simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones of a building. Both systems offer energy efficiency, quiet operation, and individual temperature control, catering to different building requirements and climate conditions.</p></p>
