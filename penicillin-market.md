<p><p>Penicillin market types encompass a wide range of antibiotics tailored to combat different types of bacterial infections. Natural Penicillins are derived from the Penicillium fungus, while Penicillinase Resistant Penicillins are designed to resist enzymes that can render traditional penicillins ineffective.
