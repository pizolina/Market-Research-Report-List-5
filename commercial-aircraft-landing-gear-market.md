<p><p>Commercial aircraft landing gear come in four main types: Strut landing gear, which is a simple and lightweight design; Rocker landing gear, which provides stability and shock absorption; Pontoon landing gear, which is commonly used on seaplanes for water landings; and Framed landing gear, which is a more complex design commonly used on larger aircraft for heavy-duty landing operations. Each type of landing gear is tailored to specific aircraft requirements and landing conditions to ensure safe and efficient landings.</p></p>
