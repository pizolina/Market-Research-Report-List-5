<p><p>Digital microsensor market can be categorized into three main types based on their applications - Chemical Microsensors, Biological Microsensors, and Physical Microsensors. Chemical microsensors are used to detect and measure various chemical substances, while biological microsensors are designed to detect biological molecules such as antigens and DNA. Physical microsensors, on the other hand, are used to measure physical parameters like pressure, temperature, and humidity. Each type of microsensor plays a crucial role in various industries including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
