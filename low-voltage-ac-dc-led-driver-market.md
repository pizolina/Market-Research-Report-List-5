<p><p>Low voltage AC-DC LED driver market types include Buck Driver, Boost Driver, and Multi-channel Driver. Buck drivers are designed to step down voltage and regulate current, resulting in efficient power conversion. Boost drivers step up voltage to deliver high power output to LEDs. Multi-channel drivers are capable of driving multiple LED channels independently. Each type serves specific purposes in LED lighting applications, offering flexibility and customization options for different lighting needs. These drivers play a crucial role in ensuring optimal performance and longevity of LED fixtures.</p></p>
