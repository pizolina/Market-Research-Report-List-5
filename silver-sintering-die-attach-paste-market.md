<p><p>Silver sintering die attach paste is used in various applications including pressure sintering and pressure-less sintering. In pressure sintering, the paste is applied between the die and substrate and compressed under high pressure to bond the components together. On the other hand, pressure-less sintering involves heating the paste at a lower pressure to achieve the bond. Both types of sintering offer unique advantages and are widely utilized in the electronics industry for their ability to create strong and reliable connections.</p></p>
