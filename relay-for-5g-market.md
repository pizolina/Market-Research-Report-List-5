<p><p>Relays for 5G come in different types, including Electromagnetic Relay and Solid State Relay (SSR). Electromagnetic relays use an electromagnet to mechanically switch power circuits on and off, suitable for controlling higher power devices. On the other hand, Solid State Relays use electronic components to switch power circuits, offering faster response times and longer lifespans. Both types of relays are crucial for ensuring reliable and efficient communication in 5G networks, each catering to specific needs and requirements within the market.</p></p>
