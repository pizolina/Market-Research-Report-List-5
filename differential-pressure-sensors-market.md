<p><p>Differential pressure sensors are categorized into two main types: Digital Type and Analog Type. Digital Type sensors provide accurate and precise measurements in a digital format, making them suitable for applications requiring high reliability and data processing capabilities. Analog Type sensors, on the other hand, deliver continuous output signals proportional to the pressure difference, making them ideal for simple monitoring and control applications. Both types offer unique features and benefits catering to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
