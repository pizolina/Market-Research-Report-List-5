<p><p>Laminated safety glass is available in two main types: polymer-based and resin-based. Polymer-based laminated safety glass is made by bonding two or more layers of glass together with a polymer interlayer, providing enhanced strength and security. Resin-based laminated safety glass, on the other hand, uses a resin interlayer for bonding the glass layers, offering similar benefits of durability and safety. Both types of laminated safety glass are widely used in automotive, construction, and other industries for their superior protective qualities.</p></p>
