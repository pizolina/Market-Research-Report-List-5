<p><strong>The Rubber Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber tires market consists of two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with layers of fabric crisscrossed at a 30-40 degree angle, providing durability and stability at lower speeds. Radial tires are built with steel belts running perpendicular to the direction of travel, allowing for better handling and fuel efficiency at higher speeds. While bias tires are often used in heavy-duty applications such as agriculture and construction, radial tires are favored in passenger cars and commercial vehicles for their performance benefits.</p></p>
