<p><p>The Bakeable Ultra-High Vacuum Valve market consists of two main types: the Gate Valve and Angle Valves. Gate Valves are ideal for applications requiring straight flow and minimal pressure drop, commonly used in high vacuum systems. On the other hand, Angle Valves are designed for applications where space is limited or where flow direction needs to be changed. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining a tight seal and controlling the flow of gases in ultra-high vacuum environments.</p></p>
