<p><p>Multifocal IOL market types include refractive and diffraction types. Refractive multifocal IOLs work by bending light to focus on multiple distances, providing clear vision at both near and far distances. On the other hand, diffraction multifocal IOLs work by separating light into different wavelengths, allowing for better distribution of light for clearer vision at varying distances. Both types of multifocal IOLs are designed to reduce the need for glasses or contact lenses after cataract surgery.</p></p>
