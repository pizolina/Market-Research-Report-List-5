<p><p>Polyester adhesive tape market consists of two main types: simple-face tape and double-sided tape. Simple-face tape has adhesive on only one side, making it ideal for applications where only one surface needs to be adhered. Double-sided tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on both sides, making it suitable for applications where two surfaces need to be joined together. Both types of tape are commonly used in various industries for bonding and mounting purposes, offering strong and durable adhesion.</p></p>
