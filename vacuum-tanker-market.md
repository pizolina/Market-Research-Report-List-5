<p><p>Vacuum tanker market types can be categorized into three main categories: liquid suctioning only, liquid and dry suctioning, and high velocity. Liquid suctioning only tankers are designed solely for the removal of liquids, such as sewage or wastewater. Tanks equipped for both liquid and dry suctioning can handle a wider range of materials, including solids like sludge or debris. High velocity tankers use powerful suction systems to quickly and efficiently remove liquids and solids from a variety of industrial settings.</p></p>
