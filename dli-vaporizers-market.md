<p><ul><li>DLI-CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition)</li><li>DLI-ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DLI Vaporizers are used in two main market types: DLI-CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and DLI-ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition). In DLI-CVD, the vaporizer is used to deposit thin films of materials onto a substrate through chemical reactions. DLI-ALD, on the other hand, is used for precise control of thin film growth by alternating the exposure of precursors on the substrate. Both market types are crucial in industries such as semiconductors, electronics, and nanotechnology for producing high-quality thin films with varying properties.</p></p>
