<p><p>Resettable PTCs fuses are classified into two main types: Polymer Type and Ceramic Type. Polymer Type resettable PTCs are made of conductive polymer materials that exhibit a positive temperature coefficient, causing them to increase resistance when heated. Conversely, Ceramic Type resettable PTCs utilize ceramic materials with similar properties. Both types offer overcurrent protection and automatically reset once the fault is cleared, making them ideal for a wide range of electronic applications. Both markets continue to grow as the demand for reliable circuit protection increases.</p></p>
