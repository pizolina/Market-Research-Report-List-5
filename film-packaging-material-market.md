<p><p>Film packaging materials can be broadly categorized into two types - rigid film packaging material and flexible film packaging material. Rigid film packaging materials are solid sheets or trays that provide a sturdy and protective barrier for products. They are often used for products that require additional support or protection during transportation and storage. Flexible film packaging materials, on the other hand, are thin and pliable materials that are ideal for products that need to conform to different shapes and sizes. They are commonly used for items like food products and consumer goods.</p></p>
