<p><p>Laser power sensors are devices used to measure the power of a laser beam and are categorized into different types based on their technology. Thermopile sensors utilize a thermoelectric device to measure laser power, while photodiode sensors rely on semiconductor materials to convert light energy into electrical current for power measurement. Other types of laser power sensors may include pyroelectric sensors, calorimeter sensors, or thermal sensors. Each type has its unique advantages and applications, catering to different measurement requirements in various industries.</p></p>
