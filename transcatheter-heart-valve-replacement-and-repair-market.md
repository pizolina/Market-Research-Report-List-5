<p><p>Transcatheter Heart Valve Replacement and Repair market includes three main types: Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR), Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve Replacement (TPVR), and Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair (TMVR). TAVR is used to replace aortic valves through a catheter, TPVR involves replacing pulmonary valves, and TMVR is used to repair or replace malfunctioning mitral valves. These minimally invasive procedures offer a less risky alternative to traditional open-heart surgery, making them increasingly popular among patients with heart valve disorders.</p></p>
