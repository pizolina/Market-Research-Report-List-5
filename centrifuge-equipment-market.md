<p><p>Centrifuge equipment market consists of two main types: vertical centrifuges and horizontal centrifuges. Vertical centrifuges operate with a rotating axis that is vertical to the ground, allowing for efficient separation of liquids and solids. Horizontal centrifuges, on the other hand, have a rotating axis parallel to the ground, making them ideal for separating liquids with different densities. Both types of centrifuges are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment for their robust performance and high efficiency.</p></p>
