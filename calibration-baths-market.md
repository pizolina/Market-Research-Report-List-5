<p><p>Calibration baths are used for calibrating temperature sensors and instruments. There are two main types of calibration baths: dry-block and liquid baths. Dry-block baths use metal blocks to evenly distribute heat, while liquid baths use a liquid medium to achieve precise temperature control. Dry-block baths are typically more portable and easier to use, while liquid baths are known for their accuracy and stability. Both types of calibration baths are essential tools in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and automotive.</p></p>
