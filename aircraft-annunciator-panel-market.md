<p><p>The Aircraft Annunciator Panel market consists of two main types: Alarm and Trouble. Alarm annunciators are designed to alert pilots to critical situations or malfunctions that require immediate attention, such as engine failure or cabin pressure loss. Trouble annunciators, on the other hand, indicate less urgent issues or maintenance reminders, like low fuel levels or a burned-out light bulb. Both types play crucial roles in helping pilots monitor the aircraft's systems and ensure safe operation during flights.</p></p>
