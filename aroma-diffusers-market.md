There are three main types of aroma diffusers on the market - Ultrasonic Aroma Diffusers, Electric Heat Aroma Diffusers, and Evaporated Aroma Diffusers. Ultrasonic diffusers use ultrasonic vibrations to break down essential oils into a fine mist, while electric heat diffusers use heat to evaporate the oils. Evaporated diffusers use a fan to disperse the oils into the air. Each type of diffuser offers a different method of dispersing essential oils for aromatherapy purposes.</p></p>
