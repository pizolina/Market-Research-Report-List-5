<p><p>Manual Steering Rack is a steering system that utilizes a manual mechanism for steering control. There are two main types of manual steering racks in the market - Rack and Pinion Type and Ball Screw Type. Rack and Pinion Type steering racks use a rotary motion to convert steering wheel input into linear motion, providing a more direct and precise steering feel. On the other hand, Ball Screw Type steering racks use ball bearings to reduce friction and provide smoother steering operation. Both types offer their own unique benefits for drivers.</p></p>
