<p><p>The automotive steering market consists of three main types: Electric Power Steering (EPS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), and other steering systems. EPS uses an electric motor to assist the driver in steering, providing a more efficient and responsive driving experience. HPS, on the other hand, uses fluid pressure from a hydraulic pump to assist in steering. The "others" category includes various steering systems such as steer-by-wire and electromechanical steering. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
