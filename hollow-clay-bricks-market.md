<p><ul><li>Vertically Perforated Clay Bricks</li><li>Horizontally Perforated Clay Bricks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow clay bricks are available in two main types: vertically perforated clay bricks and horizontally perforated clay bricks. Vertically perforated clay bricks have hollow cavities running vertically through the bricks, while horizontally perforated clay bricks have hollow sections running horizontally. Both types of bricks offer advantages such as improved thermal insulation and reduced weight compared to solid bricks. These hollow clay bricks are commonly used in construction for their structural strength, durability, and energy efficiency properties.</p></p>
