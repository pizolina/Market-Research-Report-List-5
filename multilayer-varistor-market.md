<p><p>A Multilayer Varistor is a voltage-dependent resistor that is designed to protect electronic circuits from overvoltage transients. It consists of multiple layers of zinc oxide discs sandwiched between metal electrodes. When a high voltage surge occurs, the varistor's resistance decreases, allowing it to absorb the excess energy and protect the connected devices.</p><p>The Multilayer Varistor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.30% during the forecast period.
