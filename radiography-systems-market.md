<p><p>Radiography systems market can be classified into digital and analog systems. Digital radiography systems involve the use of electronic sensors to capture images, which can be viewed and manipulated on a computer. These systems offer greater flexibility, improved image quality, and faster image processing. On the other hand, analog radiography systems use traditional film-based technology to capture images. Despite being less flexible and slower in image processing, analog systems are still widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and reliability. Both types of systems play a crucial role in medical imaging.</p></p>
