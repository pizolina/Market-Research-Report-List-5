<p><p>Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment Market includes two main types, Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) and Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD). </p><p>Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis involves the manual exchange of dialysis solution multiple times throughout the day, allowing for fluid and waste removal.</p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis, on the other hand, uses a machine to perform dialysis exchanges automatically, typically overnight while the patient sleeps. This type of dialysis offers more flexibility and convenience to patients.</p></p>
