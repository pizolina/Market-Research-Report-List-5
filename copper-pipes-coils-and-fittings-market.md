L Type is a mid-range option with medium thickness, commonly used for residential plumbing and water distribution. M Type is the thinnest and most affordable option, typically used for low-pressure applications in residential projects. Each market type offers different levels of strength, cost, and versatility to meet various project requirements.</p></p>
