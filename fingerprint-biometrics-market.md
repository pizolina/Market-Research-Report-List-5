<p><p>The Fingerprint Biometrics Market consists of two main types - AFIS Technology and Non-AFIS Technology. AFIS (Automated Fingerprint Identification System) Technology uses complex algorithms to compare and identify a fingerprint against a large database of fingerprints. Non-AFIS Technology, on the other hand, uses simpler matching techniques for basic identification and authentication purposes. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing security measures in various industries such as law enforcement, banking, and healthcare.</p></p>
