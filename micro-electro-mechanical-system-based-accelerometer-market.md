<p><strong>The Micro Electro Mechanical System Based Accelerometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1-axis</li><li>2-axis</li><li>3-axis</li><li>Combo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro Electro Mechanical System Based Accelerometers are available in various market types based on the number of axes they measure. 1-axis accelerometers measure acceleration in one direction, typically used for simple applications. 2-axis accelerometers measure acceleration in two perpendicular directions, providing more accurate data for certain applications. 3-axis accelerometers measure acceleration in all three dimensions, offering the most comprehensive data for complex applications. Combo accelerometers combine multiple axes in a single device, offering versatility and flexibility in measuring acceleration in different directions.</p></p>
