<p><ul><li>Peripheral Cutting Edge</li><li>End Cutting Edge</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small diameter end mills are commonly categorized into three types based on their cutting edge design: peripheral cutting edge, end cutting edge, and other markets. Peripheral cutting edge end mills have cutting edges on the periphery of the tool, allowing for efficient material removal along the side of the workpiece. End cutting edge end mills have cutting edges at the tip of the tool, enabling precise machining at the bottom of a pocket or slot. Other markets refer to specialized end mills designed for specific applications or materials.</p></p>
