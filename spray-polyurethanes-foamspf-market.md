<p><p>Spray Polyurethanes Foam (SPF) market is divided into three main types based on their cell structure: Open Cell, Closed Cell, and Others. Open Cell SPF is softer and less dense, providing better sound insulation and air sealing. Closed Cell SPF is more dense and provides higher R-values for better insulation. Other types include hybrid SPF combinations or specialty formulations. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of insulation properties, durability, and cost effectiveness for various construction and insulation applications.</p></p>
