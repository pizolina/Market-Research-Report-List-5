<p><p>MEMS inertial sensors market consists of three main types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Accelerometers measure acceleration forces on a sensor in three axes, while gyroscopes measure the rate of rotation on each axis. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields to determine orientation. These sensors are vital in various applications such as automotive, aerospace, consumer electronics, and industrial processes. They provide crucial data for navigation, motion tracking, image stabilization, and overall system control. The market for MEMS inertial sensors continues to grow due to increasing demand for precise motion tracking and sensing technology.</p></p>
