<p><strong>The Contact Cardiac Mapping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroanatomical mapping</li><li>Basket catheter mapping</li><li>Traditional endocardial catheter mapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact Cardiac Mapping Systems are used to create detailed images of the heart's electrical activity during cardiac procedures. There are three main types of mapping systems: Electroanatomical mapping, which combines 3D anatomical information with real-time electrical signals; Basket catheter mapping, which uses a balloon-like device to map the heart's electrical activity; and Traditional endocardial catheter mapping, which involves inserting a catheter into the heart to record electrical signals. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different clinical situations based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
