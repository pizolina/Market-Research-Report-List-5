<p><p>Immunoassay kits market includes two main types, fluorometric and colorimetric. Fluorometric immunoassay kits utilize fluorescent markers to detect and quantify specific biomolecules in a sample. This type of assay is highly sensitive and allows for precise measurements. Colorimetric immunoassay kits rely on color changes to indicate the presence of a specific biomolecule, providing a simple and cost-effective method for analysis. Both types of immunoassay kits are widely used in research, diagnostics, and drug development due to their accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
