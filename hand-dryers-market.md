<p><p>The hand dryer market can be classified into two main types: warm air hand dryers and jet-air hand dryers. Warm air hand dryers work by blowing heated air onto the hands to evaporate moisture, while jet-air hand dryers use a more powerful air stream to quickly dry hands. Warm air hand dryers are more common and cost-effective, while jet-air hand dryers are known for their faster drying times and efficiency. Both types are popular choices in commercial settings such as restrooms, airports, and offices.</p></p>
