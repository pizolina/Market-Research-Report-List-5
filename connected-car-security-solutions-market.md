<p><p>Connected car security solutions market types include V2I (vehicle-to-infrastructure), V2V (vehicle-to-vehicle), V2C (vehicle-to-cloud), V2P (vehicle-to-pedestrian), and V2X (vehicle-to-everything) solutions.
