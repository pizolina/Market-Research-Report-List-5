<p><strong>The X-Ray NDT Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR</li><li>DR</li><li>CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray NDT equipment market consists of three main types: Computed Radiography (CR), Digital Radiography (DR), and Computed Tomography (CT). </p><p>CR utilizes phosphor plates to capture X-ray images digitally, offering quick results and easy storage. </p><p>DR uses digital detectors to produce high-quality images with minimal radiation exposure. </p><p>CT technology provides detailed 3D images by rotating the X-ray source around the object being examined. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare for non-destructive testing.</p></p>
