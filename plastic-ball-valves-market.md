<p><p>Plastic ball valves come in various market types based on their connecting mechanisms. Threaded connections offer easy installation and removal, while flanged connections provide a secure and leak-proof seal. Butt weld connections are commonly used for high-pressure applications, ensuring durability and reliability. Socket weld connections are suitable for smaller pipe sizes and offer a strong, permanent bond. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences within the industry, offering a range of options for various applications.</p></p>
