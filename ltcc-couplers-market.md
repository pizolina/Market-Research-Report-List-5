<p><p>LTCC couplers are widely classified into two types based on their directional properties: directional couplers and dual-directional couplers. Directional couplers are designed to split or combine power in a specific direction, allowing for signal monitoring or power division in RF systems. On the other hand, dual-directional couplers can both split and combine signals in both directions, allowing for bi-directional power flow. Both types of couplers are essential components in RF and microwave communication systems for signal control and monitoring.</p></p>
