<p><ul><li>Cascade Type</li><li>Pivot Four Door</li><li>Bucket Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cascade Type thrust reversers redirect engine exhaust flow using a series of plates or vanes. Pivot Four Door thrust reversers have four doors that open in a pivot motion to redirect thrust. Bucket Type thrust reversers feature a clamshell-like mechanism that forms a bucket shape to redirect thrust. These different types of thrust reversers offer varying mechanisms to effectively slow down aircraft during landing by redirecting engine thrust in the opposite direction. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the aviation industry.</p></p>
