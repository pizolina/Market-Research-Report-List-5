<p><p>Spray drying towers are used for converting liquid or slurry materials into powder form. The different types of spray drying towers include Centrifugal Spray, Pressure Spray, Chinese Medicine Spray, and Air Spray. Centrifugal spray drying towers use a high-speed rotating disc to disperse the liquid material. Pressure spray towers use high pressure to atomize the liquid. Chinese medicine spray towers are specialized for drying traditional Chinese medicines. Air spray towers use compressed air to achieve atomization. Each type of spray drying tower has its own unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
