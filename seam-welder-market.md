<p><p>Seam welders are welding machines used for joining pieces of metal together along a seam. They come in three main market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual seam welders require the operator to control the welding process entirely by hand, while semi-automatic machines provide some assistance to the operator. Automatic seam welders operate independently with little to no human intervention, making them the most efficient and productive option. Each market type offers different capabilities and advantages based on the level of automation and control desired by the user.</p></p>
