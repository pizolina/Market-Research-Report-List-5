<p><strong>The Card Personalization Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Sub</li><li>Retransfer</li><li>Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card personalization services market is categorized into three types: Dye Sub, Retransfer, and Inkjet. Dye Sub printing involves heat transferring dye onto a card, resulting in high-quality images and text. Retransfer printing uses a separate film to transfer images onto the card, offering durability and edge-to-edge printing. Inkjet printing directly applies ink onto the card, suited for low volume personalization. Each type has its own unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and preferences of the customer.</p></p>
