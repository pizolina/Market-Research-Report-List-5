<p><p>The automotive oil strainer market consists of two main types: floating type and fixed type. Floating type oil strainers are designed to move along with the oil level, ensuring that only clean oil is circulating through the engine. On the other hand, fixed type oil strainers are mounted in a fixed position within the engine system. Both types play a critical role in filtering out impurities and contaminants from the oil, ensuring optimal engine performance and longevity.</p></p>
