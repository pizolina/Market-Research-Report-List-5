<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) systems automatically collect tolls from vehicles without the need for cash transactions. There are two main types of ETC systems: Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) and All Electronic Tolling (AET). ETC systems use transponders to communicate with toll booths, while AET systems rely on cameras and sensors to capture vehicle information for billing. Both systems offer a convenient and efficient way to collect tolls, reducing traffic congestion and improving overall road safety.</p></p>
