<p><ul><li>Slip-type</li><li>Core Shaft Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Casing hangers are used in oil and gas wells to support the casing strings. Slip-type casing hangers are designed to grip the casing securely, preventing it from moving longitudinally. Core shaft type casing hangers use a threaded shaft to support the casing, providing a strong connection. Both types of casing hangers play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and stability of the wellbore. The choice between slip-type and core shaft type hangers depends on factors such as well depth, pressure, and casing size.</p></p>
