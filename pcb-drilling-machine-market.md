<p><ul><li>Laser Drilling Machines</li><li>Mechanical Drilling Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB drilling machines are categorized into two main types: Laser drilling machines and Mechanical drilling machines. Laser drilling machines use high-energy laser beams to accurately drill holes in the PCB, offering precise and clean results. On the other hand, Mechanical drilling machines use rotating drill bits to create holes in the PCB. Each type of machine has its advantages and disadvantages, with laser drilling machines being preferred for their high precision and mechanical drilling machines for their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
