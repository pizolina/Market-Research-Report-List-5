<p><p>High barrier packaging includes two main types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. On the other hand, flexible packaging uses materials such as film, foil, and paper, which offer versatility in terms of shape and size. Both types of packaging are designed to maintain the freshness and quality of products by creating a barrier against moisture, oxygen, light, and other contaminants.</p></p>
