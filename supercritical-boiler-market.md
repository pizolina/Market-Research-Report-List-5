<p><p>Super critical boilers are high-efficiency boilers that operate at supercritical pressure and temperature to increase efficiency and reduce emissions. Pure DC supercritical boilers use direct circulation of water through the boiler, while compound cycle supercritical boilers use a combination of Rankine cycle and Brayton cycle to achieve higher efficiency. Both types of supercritical boilers are used in power plants to generate electricity with lower fuel consumption and reduced environmental impact compared to traditional boilers.</p></p>
