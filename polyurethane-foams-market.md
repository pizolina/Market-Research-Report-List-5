<p><p>Polyurethane foams are categorized into three main types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and spray foam. Flexible foam is commonly used in furniture, bedding, and automotive interiors due to its soft and cushioning properties. Rigid foam is used for insulation in construction and packaging applications due to its durability and thermal resistance. Spray foam is applied using a spray gun and expands to fill gaps and crevices, making it ideal for insulation and sealing in construction and automotive industries. Each type of polyurethane foam serves different purposes based on their unique properties.</p></p>
