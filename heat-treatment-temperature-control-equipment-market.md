<p><strong>The Heat Treatment Temperature Control Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ON/OFF</li><li>PID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat treatment temperature control equipment market can be divided into two main types: ON/OFF and PID. ON/OFF control systems operate by simply turning the heat source on or off based on set temperature thresholds. PID control systems, on the other hand, use a more advanced method that continuously adjusts the heat source to maintain a precise temperature. While ON/OFF systems are simpler and less costly, PID systems offer more accurate and stable temperature control, making them suitable for applications requiring tight temperature tolerances.</p></p>
