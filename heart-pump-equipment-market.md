<p><strong>The Heart Pump Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Equipment</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Implantable Heart Pump Equipment refers to devices that are surgically implanted into the body to assist with pumping blood in patients with heart failure. These devices are typically smaller and provide ongoing support for patients who are not eligible for a heart transplant. </p><p>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Equipment, on the other hand, are devices that are located outside the body and used temporarily to support circulation in critically ill patients, such as those undergoing cardiac surgery or experiencing severe heart failure. These devices are often larger and more invasive than implantable pumps.</p></p>
