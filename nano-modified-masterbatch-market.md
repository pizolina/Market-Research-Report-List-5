<p><p>The Nano Modified Masterbatch market includes two main types - PP Carrier and PE Carrier. PP Carrier masterbatch uses polypropylene as a carrier resin, whereas PE Carrier masterbatch uses polyethylene. Both types of masterbatch are modified at the nano level to enhance properties such as strength, heat resistance, and durability in the final product. PP Carrier masterbatch is commonly used in industries such as packaging and automotive, while PE Carrier masterbatch is preferred in industries like construction and agriculture.</p></p>
