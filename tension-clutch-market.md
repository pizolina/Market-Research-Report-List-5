<p><p>Tension clutches are classified into different types based on their operating mechanisms and applications. Electromagnetic tension clutches use an electromagnetic field to engage and disengage the clutch, making them suitable for precise control in various industrial applications. Friction tension clutches rely on frictional forces to transfer power between the input and output shafts, offering a simple and reliable solution for many applications. Other types of tension clutches may include pneumatic or hydraulic clutches, which provide unique benefits depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
