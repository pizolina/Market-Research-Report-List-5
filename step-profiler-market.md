<p><ul><li>Inductive</li><li>Piezo</li><li>Photoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Step profiler market includes inductive, piezo, and photoelectric types. Inductive step profilers operate based on electromagnetic induction and provide accurate measurements with high resolution. Piezo step profilers use piezoelectric technology to measure step heights in nanometer scale, offering high precision and sensitivity. Photoelectric step profilers utilize light emissions to detect step heights with exceptional resolution and speed. Each type caters to specific needs and applications, providing a range of options for industries requiring precise and reliable step profiling capabilities.</p></p>
