<p><p>Flame resistant fabrics can be categorized into three main types based on their method of fire resistance. Treated flame resistant fabrics are treated with chemicals to enhance their flame resistance. Hybrid flame resistant fabrics combine both inherent and treated properties to provide an extra layer of protection against flames. Each type of fabric offers varying levels of protection and is used in different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
